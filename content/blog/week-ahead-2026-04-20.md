TSLA printed $32M in call accumulation last week. AAPL and GOOGL stacked behind it. Friday closed with three large institutional prints — all calls — across mega-cap tech and semis. The market is walking into a heavy earnings week with institutional positioning already in motion.

## What Institutions Positioned Into the Week

TSLA drew $32M in call accumulation across 262 signals last week. One contract saw 10 repeat entries — that kind of [options accumulation](/blog/what-is-options-accumulation) is rarely noise. The lean was calls throughout.

AAPL logged $15.1M in call-side flow across 6 signals. Max 9 hits on a single contract. Quiet by signal count — not by dollar weight.

GOOGL ran $9.8M in call accumulation across 10 signals, with one contract seeing 12 repeat entries. That contract stood out. GOOGL also showed up in Friday's Grade A prints — the same name, two sessions of positioning.

Several other tickers saw comparable patterns last week. Those contracts are tracked in the live feed.
